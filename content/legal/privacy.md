# Privacy Policy

This Privacy Policy explains how **AP Practice Exam Online** ("**we**," "**us**," or "**our**") collects, uses, discloses, and protects personal data when you use our website, applications, and related services (the "**Service**"). It applies to visitors, registered users, moderators, and anyone who interacts with us.

**Last updated:** July 2026 · **Version:** 2.0

We process personal data in accordance with applicable laws, including the EU General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR), Türkiye Personal Data Protection Law (KVKK), U.S. state privacy laws (including CCPA/CPRA), and regional laws in the Middle East and North Africa (MENA) where relevant.

For cookie-specific information, see our [Cookie Policy](/cookies). For terms of use, see our [Terms of Service](/terms).

## AI Processing and PDF Content

When you consent to **AI processing**, we may transmit portions of your uploaded PDFs and derived text to **Google Gemini** and/or **Anthropic Claude** to extract questions, analyze content, and score responses.

**Important considerations:**

- PDFs may contain copyrighted or third-party material; you are responsible for lawful uploads (see [Terms of Service](/terms)).
- AI providers process data under their own terms and may use infrastructure in multiple countries.
- We instruct processors to handle data for extraction/scoring purposes; we do not use your private uploads to train public foundation models unless we explicitly disclose otherwise and obtain consent.
- AI output may be inaccurate; it is for practice only.

You must provide **per-session or per-action consent** where our product requires it. Consent events are stored in our `user_consents` records with policy version, legal region, timestamp, and hashed IP/user-agent for audit.

## Publishing, Moderation, and Public Visibility

By default, uploads are **private**. If you choose to **publish** an exam:

- You provide explicit **public_publish** consent;
- Submissions may be reviewed by **moderators** before appearing publicly;
- Approved exams display **source attribution** to other users;
- Moderator decisions, rejection reasons, and unpublish actions may be logged;
- Published content may be copied or viewed by other users **within the Service** for personal study.

If you delete your Account or unpublish content, we remove or de-index public displays in accordance with our retention practices, subject to backup lag and legal holds.
