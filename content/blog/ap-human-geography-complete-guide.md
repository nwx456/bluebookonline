The 2026 AP Human Geography exam is **Tuesday, May 5, 2026 (8 AM local)** — often a student's first AP exam.

## Exam Structure

Fully digital — **2 hours 15 minutes**.

| Section | Questions | Weight |
|---|---|---|
| Section I (MCQ) | 60 | 50% |
| Section II (FRQ) | 3 (7 pts each) | 50% |

## Unit Weights

All content units (2–7) are weighted **12–17%** each. Unit 1 (Thinking Geographically) is **8–10%**.

| Unit | Topic |
|---|---|
| 1 | Thinking Geographically |
| 2 | Population and Migration |
| 3 | Cultural Patterns |
| 4 | Political Patterns |
| 5 | Agriculture and Rural Land-Use |
| 6 | Cities and Urban Land-Use |
| 7 | Industrial and Economic Development |

## FRQ Tips

- **Define then apply** — name the model, explain it, apply to the stimulus.
- **Specific examples** — "Germany" beats "a European country."
- **Three FRQs in 75 minutes** — budget ~25 min each.

## Key Models to Master

- Demographic Transition Model
- Epidemiological Transition
- Von Thünen Model
- Burgess Concentric Zone Model
- Rostow's Stages of Growth
