The 2026 AP Microeconomics exam is **Monday, May 4, 2026 (12 PM local)**.

## Exam Structure

Hybrid digital — **2 hours 10 minutes**.

| Section | Questions | Weight |
|---|---|---|
| Section I (MCQ) | 60 | ~67% |
| Section II (FRQ) | 3 (10+5+5 pts) | ~33% |

Section I in Bluebook; FRQ prompts on screen, answers in paper booklet.

## Unit Weights

| Unit | Topic | Weight |
|---|---|---|
| 1 | Basic Economic Concepts | 12–15% |
| 2 | Supply and Demand | 20–25% |
| 3 | Production, Cost, Perfect Competition | 22–25% |
| 4 | Imperfect Competition | 15–22% |
| 5 | Factor Markets | 10–13% |
| 6 | Market Failure and Government | 8–13% |

**Units 2–4 = ~57–72%** — master supply/demand shifts and all four market structures.

## FRQ Tips

- **Label everything** — axes, curves, equilibrium points, areas of consumer/producer surplus.
- **Long FRQ (10 pts)** — often multi-part market analysis or policy evaluation.
- **Short FRQs (5 pts each)** — quick graph or calculation.

## Study Plan

**Weeks 1–2:** Units 1–2 (supply/demand, elasticity, consumer/producer surplus).

**Weeks 3–4:** Units 3–4 (cost curves, monopoly, oligopoly, monopolistic competition).

**Weeks 5–6:** Units 5–6 + released FRQs from AP Central.
