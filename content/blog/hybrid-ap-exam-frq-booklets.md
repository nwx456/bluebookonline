Twelve AP exams in 2026 use the **hybrid digital format**: multiple-choice in Bluebook, free-response prompts on screen, answers **handwritten in paper booklets**. If you're taking AP Calculus, Biology, Chemistry, Physics, Statistics, Economics, or Precalculus, this guide is essential.

## How Hybrid Digital Testing Works

Per [College Board's hybrid digital overview](https://apcentral.collegeboard.org/exam-administration-ordering-scores/administering-exams/digital-ap-exams/hybrid-digital):

1. **Section I:** Complete all MCQs in Bluebook (same tools: highlight, eliminate, mark-for-review)
2. **Section II transition:** Proctor distributes **personalized FRQ booklets** with your AP ID label
3. **Section II:** Read FRQ prompts in Bluebook; **write all scored work in the booklet**
4. **Submission:** MCQ auto-submits; proctor collects booklets for mailing to AP scoring

See the full [2026 format list](/blog/fully-digital-vs-hybrid-ap-exams).

## What's on the FRQ Booklet Cover

Every hybrid subject's booklet front cover includes:

- Student identification fields (completed before testing begins)
- **Number of FRQ questions** to answer in the booklet
- **Section II duration**
- Pen/pencil permission (varies slightly by subject)

Proctors read directions aloud; Bluebook also displays on-screen instructions throughout Section II.

## Subject-Specific Booklet Differences

College Board provides **full-length sample booklets** at [cb.org/ap-digital-hybrid](https://cb.org/ap-digital-hybrid):

| Subject | FRQ count | Notable format |
|---|---|---|
| AP Calculus AB/BC | 6 | Multi-part questions; show all work |
| AP Biology | 6 (2 long + 4 short) | Long FRQs need labeled diagrams |
| AP Chemistry | 7 | Updated 2026 format — see College Board sample |
| AP Physics 1/2/C | 5 or 3 | Show equations, units, significant figures |
| AP Statistics | 6 | Investigative Task (Q6) is often score differentiator |
| AP Micro/Macro | 3 | Graph-heavy; label axes clearly |
| AP Precalculus | 4 | Mixed calculator/no-calculator parts |

**March 2026 update:** College Board posted an updated AP Chemistry sample booklet aligned with the February 2026 AP Classroom practice exam.

## Rules That Cost Students Points

- **All scored work must be in the booklet** — scratch paper is for planning only
- **Don't write in the wrong booklet section** — follow Bluebook's question numbering
- **Show work for partial credit** — especially Calc and Physics
- **Units and significant figures** matter on science FRQs
- **Calculator sections** are specified in Bluebook — don't use calculator on prohibited parts

## Fully Digital vs Hybrid: Why It Matters

Students often prepare for typed FRQs but test with **handwriting** on hybrid exams. Conversely, history students who practiced handwriting are surprised by **typed essays**.
