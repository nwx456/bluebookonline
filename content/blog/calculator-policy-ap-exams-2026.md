**Short answer:** Calculator rules **vary by AP subject** — Calculus, Statistics, Chemistry, Physics, and Economics allow approved graphing or four-function calculators; CSA, English, History, and Gov exams do **not**.

## 2026 Calculator Policy Table

| AP Subject | Calculator allowed? | Type |
|---|---|---|
| Calculus AB/BC | Yes | Graphing recommended |
| Statistics | Yes | Graphing required |
| Chemistry | Yes | Scientific/graphing |
| Physics 1/2/C | Yes | Scientific/graphing |
| Micro/Macro Economics | Yes | Four-function |
| Biology | Yes | Four-function, scientific, or graphing |
| Precalculus | Yes | Graphing |
| Environmental Science | No (exam) | — |
| CSA / CSP | No | — |
| All History & English | No | — |
| Psychology / Human Geo / Gov | No | — |
