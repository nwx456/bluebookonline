**Short answer:** The Digital SAT has **98 scored questions** — **54 Reading & Writing** (two modules × 27) and **44 Math** (two modules × 22), ~**2 hours 14 minutes** total.

## Full Question Count Table

| Section | Module 1 | Module 2 | Section total | Time |
|---|---|---|---|---|
| Reading & Writing | 27 | 27 (adaptive) | **54** | 64 min |
| Math | 22 | 22 (adaptive) | **44** | 70 min |
| **Total** | 49 | 49 | **98** | ~134 min |

## Module 2 Adaptive Routing

Module 1 performance determines whether Module 2 is **easier or harder**, which affects your score ceiling. See [how adaptive testing works](/blog/how-digital-sat-adaptive-testing-works).

## Question Types

- **R&W:** All four-option multiple choice (A–D).
- **Math:** Multiple choice + [grid-in questions](/blog/sat-grid-in-student-produced-response-guide) (~11 grid-in per full test).
