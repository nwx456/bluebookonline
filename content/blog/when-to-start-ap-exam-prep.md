**Short answer:** Start **light AP review in January**, **intensive prep in March**, and **full timed practice tests in April** — earlier for self-study or early May exams like Human Geography.

## Month-by-Month Timeline

| Month | Focus |
|---|---|
| **Sep–Dec** | Keep up in class; flashcards for vocab-heavy APs |
| **January** | Diagnostic practice test; identify weak units |
| **February** | Unit drills + first FRQ practice |
| **March** | Timed section practice; review [exam dates](/blog/ap-exam-dates-2026) |
| **April** | 2+ full-length timed tests per subject |
| **May 1–exam** | Light review only; sleep and logistics |

## Early May Exams

These sit in the first week — start intensive prep **two weeks earlier**:

- AP Human Geography (May 5)
- AP Microeconomics (May 4 PM)
