**Short answer:** Colleges use AP scores for **admissions context** (rigor validation) and separately for **credit/placement** — reporting is optional, and credit thresholds vary from 3 to 5 by school and subject.

## Admissions vs Registrar

| Office | How AP scores are used |
|---|---|
| **Admissions** | Confirms you challenged yourself; 4s/5s strengthen STEM/humanities claims |
| **Registrar / credit** | Applies published AP credit chart after enrollment |

Admissions readers weigh **course grades + course rigor** first. AP scores are supporting evidence.

## When to Report AP Scores

**Report** if most scores are 3+ and align with your intended major.

**Consider withholding** 1s and 2s unless required — use College Board score withholding/cancel options.
