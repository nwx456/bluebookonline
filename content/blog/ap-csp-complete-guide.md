The 2026 AP CSP end-of-course exam is **Thursday, May 14, 2026 (12 PM local)**. The **Create Performance Task is due Thursday, April 30, 2026**.

## Exam Structure

Fully digital — **2 hours** (MCQ section) plus Create PT submitted separately.

| Component | Weight |
|---|---|
| Section I (70 MCQs) | 70% |
| Create Performance Task (0–6 rubric) | 30% |

## Big Idea Weights

| Big Idea | Topic | Weight |
|---|---|---|
| 1 | Creative Development | 10–13% |
| 2 | Data | 17–22% |
| 3 | Algorithms and Programming | 30–35% |
| 4 | Computer Systems and Networks | 11–15% |
| 5 | Impact of Computing | 21–26% |

**Big Ideas 3 + 5 = ~51–61%** — algorithms and computing impact dominate.

## Create PT Checklist

- Working program that solves a problem
- Video (≤1 min) showing input, output, and functionality
- Written responses explaining code, data, and development process
- Submit through AP Digital Portfolio by **April 30**

## Study Plan

**Weeks 1–2:** Big Ideas 2–3 (data representation, algorithms, programming logic).

**Weeks 3–4:** Big Ideas 4–5 (internet, cybersecurity, impact of computing).

**Week 5:** Create PT finalization and submission.

**Week 6:** MCQ review — practice binary, algorithms, and internet protocol questions.
