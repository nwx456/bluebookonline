In 2026, **most AP exams are digital** — but "digital" doesn't mean the same thing for every subject. College Board splits Bluebook exams into two formats: **fully digital** (everything in the app) and **hybrid digital** (MCQ in Bluebook, FRQs handwritten on paper).

This guide lists every 2026 exam by format, explains what changes on test day, and links to practice tools matched to each format.

## 2026 AP Exam Format Overview

Per the [College Board exam modes page](https://apcentral.collegeboard.org/exam-administration-ordering-scores/administering-exams/digital-ap-exams/exam-modes):

| Format | Count | What students do |
|---|---|---|
| **Fully digital** | 16 exams | MCQ + FRQ completed in Bluebook; auto-submitted |
| **Hybrid digital** | 12 exams | MCQ in Bluebook; FRQs viewed on screen, **written in paper booklets** |
| **Other formats** | 12 exams | Portfolio, spoken, or paper-based (not in Bluebook) |

## Fully Digital AP Exams (16)

Students complete **both multiple-choice and free-response in Bluebook**. Responses submit automatically at exam end.

- AP African American Studies*
- AP Art History
- AP Comparative Government and Politics
- AP Computer Science A
- AP Computer Science Principles*
- AP English Language and Composition
- AP English Literature and Composition
- AP Environmental Science
- AP European History
- AP Human Geography
- AP Latin*
- AP Psychology
- AP Seminar*
- AP United States Government and Politics
- AP United States History
- AP World History: Modern

*\*Also has AP Digital Portfolio components.*

**Practice tip:** For these exams, practice **typing FRQs** under time pressure. Use our [FRQ upload module](/dashboard/upload?kind=frq) to simulate typed responses with AI rubric grading.

## Hybrid Digital AP Exams (12)

Students complete **MCQ in Bluebook**, view FRQ prompts on screen, and **handwrite answers in paper booklets** returned for scoring.

- AP Biology
- AP Calculus AB
- AP Calculus BC
- AP Chemistry
- AP Macroeconomics
- AP Microeconomics
- AP Physics 1: Algebra-Based
- AP Physics 2: Algebra-Based
- AP Physics C: Electricity and Magnetism
- AP Physics C: Mechanics
- AP Precalculus
- AP Statistics

**Practice tip:** MCQ practice on [Bluebook-style tests](/exams) is critical. For FRQs, use paper practice with [official sample booklets](https://apcentral.collegeboard.org/exam-administration-ordering-scores/administering-exams/digital-ap-exams/hybrid-digital). Read our [hybrid FRQ booklet guide](/blog/hybrid-ap-exam-frq-booklets).

## Exams NOT in Bluebook (12)

These use other administration formats (spoken responses, portfolios, paper exams):

- AP 2-D Art and Design, 3-D Art and Design, Drawing (portfolio)
- AP French, German, Italian, Japanese, Spanish Language, Spanish Literature (include spoken components)
- AP Music Theory (includes sight-singing)
- AP Research (presentation + portfolio)

## Key Differences on Test Day

| Feature | Fully digital | Hybrid digital |
|---|---|---|
| FRQ input | Typed in Bluebook | Handwritten in booklet |
| Submission | Automatic at end | MCQ auto; booklets collected |
| Tools | Highlight, mark-for-review, eliminate | Same for MCQ; pen/pencil for FRQs |
| Practice focus | Typing speed + essay structure | MCQ pacing + handwritten FRQ clarity |
