**Short answer:** **AP exam registration** happens **through your school in fall 2025** (typically **October–November ordering**); confirm your coordinator’s deadline and see [May 2026 exam dates](/blog/ap-exam-dates-2026).

## Registration Timeline

| When | Action |
|---|---|
| **Aug–Sep 2025** | Join AP sections in My AP / AP Classroom |
| **Oct–Nov 2025** | School orders exams; students confirm intent |
| **After Nov 2025** | Late ordering fees may apply |
| **Spring 2026** | Exam day logistics, Bluebook readiness checks |
| **May 4–15, 2026** | AP Exam administration |

Exact dates vary by school — ask your AP coordinator.
