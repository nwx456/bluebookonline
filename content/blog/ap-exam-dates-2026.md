Official schedule source: [College Board AP Exam Calendar](https://apstudents.collegeboard.org/exam-calendar).

## Format Reminders

- **Fully digital:** History, English, Gov, Human Geo, ES, CSA, CSP — typed FRQs in Bluebook
- **Hybrid digital:** Calc, Bio, Chem, Physics, Econ, Stats — MCQ in Bluebook, handwritten FRQs

See [fully digital vs hybrid](/blog/fully-digital-vs-hybrid-ap-exams) for the complete list.
