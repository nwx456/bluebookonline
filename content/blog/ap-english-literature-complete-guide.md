**Short answer:** The 2026 AP English Literature and Composition exam is Wed, May 6, 2026 (8 AM Local) — 55 MCQs, 180 minutes total, fully digital in Bluebook.

The 2026 AP English Literature exam is **Wednesday, May 6, 2026 (8 AM local)**. Pass rate ~74% in 2025.

## Exam Structure

Fully digital — **3 hours**.

| Section | Questions | Weight |
|---|---|---|
| Section I (MCQ) | 55 | 45% |
| Section II (Essays) | 3 (6 pts each) | 55% |

### The Three Essays

1. **Poetry Analysis** — analyze how poetic elements create meaning.
2. **Prose Fiction Analysis** — analyze narrative techniques in a passage.
3. **Literary Argument** — develop a thesis about a work of literary merit you choose.

Essays are **55% of your score** — two strong essays can lift a weak MCQ section.

## Course Structure

Nine units cycling through short fiction, poetry, and longer fiction/drama at increasing complexity. No fixed unit percentages on the exam — skills matter more than unit labels.

## Essay Rubric (0–6)

- **Thesis (1 pt):** Defensible interpretation, not plot summary.
- **Evidence & Commentary (4 pts):** Specific textual evidence with line-by-line analysis.
- **Sophistication (1 pt):** Complex understanding of literary context or nuance.

## Study Plan

**Weeks 1–2:** Poetry — scansion, tone, imagery, structure. Write 5 poetry essays.

**Weeks 3–4:** Prose — narrative voice, characterization, symbolism.

**Weeks 5–6:** Literary Argument — prepare 3–4 works you know deeply (novels/plays).
