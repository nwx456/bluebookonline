The 2026 AP Physics C: Electricity and Magnetism exam is **Thursday, May 14, 2026 (12 PM local)**.

## Exam Structure

40 MCQs (50%) + 4 FRQs (50%). Same four FRQ archetypes as Mechanics (10+12+10+8 points).

## Unit Weights

| Unit | Topic | Weight |
|---|---|---|
| 1 | Electric Charges, Fields, Gauss's Law | 20–25% |
| 2 | Electric Potential | 15–20% |
| 3 | Conductors and Capacitors | 12–17% |
| 4 | Electric Circuits | 17–22% |
| 5 | Magnetic Fields and Electromagnetism | 15–20% |
| 6 | Electromagnetic Induction | 13–18% |

Units 1, 4, and 6 are critical — Gauss's law, RC/LC circuits, and Faraday's law appear every year.

## FRQ Tips

- Set up **Gauss's law** integrals with correct symmetry.
- For circuits: Kirchhoff's rules + calculus (capacitor charging/discharging).
- Show **Lenz's law direction** before calculating induced EMF.
