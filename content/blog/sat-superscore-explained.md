**Superscoring** is one of the most practical retake strategies for the Digital SAT — yet many students do not understand how it works until after their second test. This guide explains superscore mechanics, which colleges use it, and how to plan retakes strategically.

> **Related:** [Score percentiles](/blog/sat-score-percentiles-2026) · [Improve 100 points](/blog/how-to-improve-sat-score-100-points) · [Score calculator](/tools/sat-score-calculator)

## How Superscoring Works

Suppose you take the SAT twice:

| Sitting | R&W | Math | Total |
|---|---|---|---|
| March | 640 | 720 | 1360 |
| May | 680 | 690 | 1370 |

Your **single-sitting best** is 1370 (May). Your **superscore** is 680 + 720 = **1400** — combining May R&W with March Math.

Colleges that superscore use 1400 for admission decisions and often for merit aid.

## Who Superscores?

Superscoring is a **college policy**, not a College Board feature. Common patterns:

- **Most selective private universities** superscore (e.g., many in the Ivy League, Stanford, MIT)
- **Many state flagships** superscore for admission
- **Some colleges** use highest single sitting only — always verify on the college's admissions website

Check [College Board's BigFuture](https://bigfuture.collegeboard.org) or each college's admissions page for current policy.

## Retake Strategy for Superscore Schools

If your target colleges superscore:

1. **Identify your weaker section** using the [score calculator](/tools/sat-score-calculator).
2. **Retake focused on that section** — but remember both sections are tested every sitting.
3. **Do not neglect your strong section** — a 50-point drop in your best section wastes a retake.

### Example Plan

Starting score: R&W 600, Math 700 (1300 total)

- **Retake focus:** R&W grammar and inference drills
- **Maintain:** Math with one practice module per week
- **Target superscore:** R&W 660 + Math 700 = **1360**

## Superscore vs Score Choice

- **Superscore:** College combines your best sections (their policy)
- **Score Choice:** You choose which sittings to send (College Board feature)

You can use Score Choice to send only sittings that help your superscore. Colleges still only superscore from scores you send.

## Digital SAT and Superscoring

The Digital SAT superscores the same way as the paper SAT — best R&W + best Math across sittings. The adaptive format does not change superscore mechanics.

## When NOT to Retake

- Your superscore already meets your target college's middle 50%
- You have taken the test 3+ times with minimal improvement
- Test fatigue is hurting your strong section
