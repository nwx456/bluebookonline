The 2026 AP European History exam is **Monday, May 4, 2026 (12 PM local)**.

## Exam Structure

Fully digital — **3 hours 15 minutes**.

| Section | Component | Weight |
|---|---|---|
| Section IA | 55 MCQs | 40% |
| Section IB | 3 SAQs (9 pts combined) | 20% |
| Section IIA | 1 DBQ (7 pts) | 25% |
| Section IIB | 1 LEQ (6 pts) | 15% |

## Unit Weights

All 9 units are weighted **10–15%** each — no single unit dominates.

| Units | Period |
|---|---|
| 1–2 | Renaissance through Reformation |
| 3–4 | Absolutism through Enlightenment |
| 5–6 | 18th-century crisis through Industrialization |
| 7–9 | 19th century through Cold War and contemporary Europe |
