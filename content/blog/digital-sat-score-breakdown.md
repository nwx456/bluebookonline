**Short answer:** The Digital SAT reports **Reading & Writing (200–800)** + **Math (200–800)** = **total 400–1600**, each built from **two adaptive modules** with no wrong-answer penalty.

## Score Structure

```
Total (400–1600)
├── Reading & Writing (200–800)
│   ├── Module 1: 27 questions, 32 min
│   └── Module 2: 27 questions, 32 min (adaptive)
└── Math (200–800)
    ├── Module 1: 22 questions, 35 min
    └── Module 2: 22 questions, 35 min (adaptive)
```

## Adaptive Module 2

Strong Module 1 → harder Module 2 → higher score potential. See [adaptive testing guide](/blog/how-digital-sat-adaptive-testing-works).
